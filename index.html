About Me

Jeremy is a Ph.D. student at the University of Michigan studying Computer Science and Engineering with a focus on computer security. He received his M.S. in Computer Science from the University of California, Davis, with a focus on Android security and privacy. He has previously been a researcher / developer / teacher at Sandia National Laboratories in California where he led the Center for Cyber Defenders internship program for several years and co-founded the Cyber Technologies Academy. He also worked on a bunch of other projects, like FARM and minimega. He is known for sending embarrassing emails from any unattended and unlocked computer. You've been warned.
